FAQ

Common Questions

How long does a NetSuite implementation take? ▼
Most implementations take 8–16 weeks depending on complexity, number of modules, and company size. Simple setups can be done in 6 weeks; multi-subsidiary global rollouts may take 6+ months.
What does a NetSuite implementation cost? ▼
Costs vary by scope. As a freelancer, my rates are significantly lower than agencies — typically 40–60% less. I provide detailed, fixed-price proposals after a free discovery call so there are no surprises.
Can you work with my existing NetSuite account? ▼
Absolutely. Whether you're implementing from scratch, rescuing a failed project, or optimising an existing account, I can assess and take over at any stage.
Do you offer post-implementation support? ▼
Yes. I offer flexible monthly retainer packages for ongoing support, enhancements, and user assistance. Most clients move to a support arrangement after go-live.
Are you available for clients outside India? ▼
Yes — I work with clients globally. I've delivered projects for businesses in Australia, the US, UK, and across Asia Pacific. I accommodate time zones for key meetings.
