Shopify ↔ NetSuite
Real-Time Sync

How a $10M e-commerce brand eliminated 12+ hours of weekly manual work with a bidirectional real-time integration.

The Challenge

A rapidly growing Shopify store processing $10M+ in annual revenue was drowning in manual data entry. Orders from Shopify had to be manually entered into NetSuite, inventory updates were being done by hand twice daily, and refunds required three separate system updates to reconcile properly.

The operations team was spending 12+ hours every week on reconciliation tasks. Errors were causing inventory discrepancies, overselling, and delayed fulfilment. Month-end close took 3 days longer than it should have.

  • Orders from Shopify not syncing to NetSuite in real time
  • Inventory updates manually done — causing overselling incidents
  • Refunds required manual updates in both systems
  • Customer records duplicated across platforms
  • Month-end reconciliation taking 3 extra days

The Solution

I designed and built a fully automated, bidirectional integration between Shopify and NetSuite using a combination of Shopify webhooks, a custom NetSuite RESTlet, and a Map/Reduce script for bulk processing.

The architecture was designed for resilience — with retry logic, error alerting, and a reconciliation job that catches any gaps from webhook failures.

Key integration points built:

  • Order sync: Shopify orders → NetSuite Sales Orders in real time via webhook + RESTlet
  • Inventory sync: NetSuite inventory updates → Shopify via Scheduled Script every 15 minutes
  • Refund sync: Shopify refund webhooks → NetSuite Credit Memos automatically
  • Customer sync: Bidirectional customer record sync with deduplication logic
  • Reconciliation job: Nightly Map/Reduce script catches any missed webhook events

The Results

The integration went live in week 6 of the project and the impact was immediate. The operations team got back their 12+ hours per week, the inventory accuracy jumped to 99.9%, and the month-end close time was cut in half.

  • 12+ hours saved weekly — operations team fully redeployed to growth tasks
  • 99.9% sync accuracy — zero overselling incidents since go-live (18+ months)
  • Real-time order visibility — fulfilment team sees NetSuite orders within seconds of Shopify checkout
  • Month-end close time reduced by 60% — no manual reconciliation required
  • Zero manual data entry — across orders, inventory, refunds, and customers
