Rollout for Manufacturer

End-to-end NetSuite implementation for a 200-person manufacturing company — delivered in 6 months, on time and under budget.

The Challenge

A mid-sized precision parts manufacturer with 200 employees was running on a legacy ERP system that was over 12 years old — no longer supported, with no mobile access, and completely disconnected from their warehouse and production floor systems.

The company's rapid growth had exposed the limits of their old system: production orders weren't syncing with inventory, procurement was done via spreadsheets, and month-end financial reporting took two weeks to compile manually.

  • Legacy ERP with no vendor support or updates since 2019
  • Inventory and production data siloed in separate spreadsheets
  • Procurement managed entirely via email and Excel
  • Financial reporting taking 2 weeks each month to compile
  • No real-time visibility across 3 warehouse locations
  • Manual data re-entry between systems causing costly errors

The Results

Six months after go-live, the impact was clear across every department. Production, finance, and warehouse teams were all working from a single source of truth for the first time in the company's history.

  • 40% improvement in operational efficiency — measured across procurement, production, and fulfilment cycles
  • Month-end close reduced from 14 days to 4 days — real-time financial data made reporting instant
  • 200+ users successfully onboarded — across 3 locations with zero rollback incidents
  • Inventory accuracy improved from 78% to 98.5% — real-time WMS eliminated stockouts and overstock
  • Procurement cycle time cut by 50% — automated 3-way PO matching replaced manual approvals
  • Delivered on time and 8% under budget
